African Conference on Artificial Intelligence 2023

Head of States, Ministers and officials of AI, researchers, policy makers among other are expected to participate in the conference. conference has identified six thematic areas as its primary focuses including public services, cyber-security, agriculture, public health, geographic information system and Trustworthy and Ethical. Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is defined as the simulation of human intelligence to perform various tasks through a computer or machine-based system
